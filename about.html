Gaming Discovery Platform

About MetaPlay

MetaPlay is your go-to platform for discovering games that are truly worth your time. We bring together expert critic reviews, community scores, and rich game metadata - all in one clean, fast, and beautiful interface.

Whether you're looking for the next masterpiece RPG, a chill indie platformer, or the top-rated competitive shooter - MetaPlay helps you cut through the noise and find games that match your taste. No ads, no paid placements. Just honest scores.

What is MetaPlay?

A gaming discovery platform that aggregates critic reviews and community ratings into a single Metascore. Built for gamers who want to find quality games, fast.

Our Mission

To make game discovery transparent, honest, and enjoyable. We believe every gamer deserves to know what they're getting into before spending their time or money.

For Every Gamer

From hardcore Souls-like fans to casual mobile gamers - MetaPlay covers all genres and platforms. Our database spans decades of gaming history.

Technologies Used

Built entirely with HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript - no frameworks. Uses localStorage for wishlists, reviews, and user accounts. Fully responsive.

Key Features

Platform filtering, real-time search, game detail pages with trailers, critic and user reviews, personal wishlist, dark/light mode, and user accounts.
